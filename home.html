Amakuru y’ingenzi

Uko worora neza

Ubworozi bw’inka busaba kubitaho neza, kuzitunga ku mafunguro meza kandi zujuje isuku.

Indyo y’inka

Inka zigomba kugaburirwa ibyuzuye harimo ibyatsi bishya, amazi meza n'ibiryo byongera umusaruro.

Ibyiza by’ubworozi

Ubworozi bufasha mu kubona amata, ifumbire, inyama ndetse n’amafaranga avuye ku kugurisha.
